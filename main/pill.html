Never Forget Your Pills Again

Pill Reminder is a simple and effective app to help you stay on top of your medication schedule.

Get Started
Easy to Use

Pill Reminder is designed to be simple and intuitive, so you can start using it right away.

Customizable

You can set up reminders for all your medication, with customizable settings for each one.

Reliable

With Pill Reminder, you can be confident that you'll never miss a dose again.

How It Works

  1. Download the Pill Reminder app from the App Store or Google Play.
  2. Enter your medication schedule and preferences.
  3. Get reminders when it's time to take your pills.
  4. Mark each dose as taken or skipped.
  5. View your medicationhistory and share it with your healthcare provider.
